## Claims invalidated from prior rounds

1. "Entry-level down 25%" → actual: -20%, US-only, 0–2 YOE. Candidate unaffected.
2. "79% Claude Code automation = devs cooked" → misread.
3. "48% Q1 2026 layoffs AI-attributed" → actual 5% full-year, ~13% 2026 YTD.
4. "Remote-US contractor = clean upside" → net comp post-AT-tax: €70–85K from $150–180K gross. Ceiling real, floor much lower than headline.
5. Scoring underweighted .NET (139 AT postings, Mangelberuf) and overweighted AI engineer (80 AT postings, thin).

## New findings

- Salzburg-local as a market = 31 jobs total. Not a market.
- Mid-level devs in DACH not in layoff crisis — in raise-freeze. Risk = stagnation, not unemployment.
- AT Stepstone Senior .NET P50 €63K — below candidate's likely scope of work.

## Recommendation at time (round 03)

Stay .NET-primary, add "AI-augmented / agentic experience" as differentiating layer, search Vienna + remote-DACH, target €75–85K in 12 months. Full AI pivot = variance too high for mid-level with income obligations.
