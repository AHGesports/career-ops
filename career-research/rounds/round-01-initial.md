- Glassdoor AT, WeAreDevelopers, WorldSalaries

## Findings (as stated at the time)

- Candidate is "underpricing by 20–40%"; Austria senior full-stack median ~€70K; profile fits top decile at age 22.
- Remote-US contractor proposed as "highest-dollar play" at €100–180K cash.
- Recommended 3 tracks: Agentic AI Engineer (top), AI Platform/Infra (highest ceiling), Founding Engineer at AI-native startup.
- Proposed skills to add: LLM evals + observability, K8s + Terraform, AWS SA-Pro cert, Go.
- Proposed skip: Rust, Elixir, Web3, second cloud cert, React migration.

## What this round got right (validated later)

- Agentic AI Engineer as a strong direction for this candidate's profile.
- K8s + cloud + Terraform as durable skill additions.
- Austrian market comp is compressed relative to remote-EU and remote-US.

## What this round got wrong (invalidated by round 02–03)

- Treated "AI engineer" as a volume path; data later showed it's a prestige path with low AT volume (80 postings).
- Over-weighted remote-US contractor ceiling without tax-reality modeling.
- Cited "entry-level down 25%" which is Stanford data for 22–25yo, 0–2 YOE, US-only — candidate has 4 YOE.
- Proposed skill list was generic; did not integrate candidate's existing Elyt portfolio as a specific differentiator.
