## What this round got right (validated later)

## What this round got wrong (invalidated by round 03)

- "79% Claude Code automation" — misread: that's power-user delegation stat, not dev-job automation.
- "Q1 layoffs 48% AI-attributed" — Challenger actual is 5% full-year 2025, peak 25% March 2025, ~13% 2026 YTD. Number was wrong.
- "Entry-level down 25%" — Stanford paper says -20% for 22–25yo 0–2 YOE US-only. Candidate's 4 YOE is in the growing cohort.
- Remote-US contractor presented as unqualified upside; tax/social reality not modeled.
- Elyt treated as unconditional asset; traction not verified.
