Pre-committed triggers that cause a switch in strategy. Each specifies: the trigger, the action, and the reasoning.

## Elyt triggers

### T-01 — Elyt can't show measurable traction or OSS signal by Month 2 (June 2026)
**Action:** Demote Elyt in CV to one bullet; don't lead with it. Option M score drops from 8.1 → ~7.5 (still #1 but margin narrows).
**Reasoning:** Founder-claim without proof reads as a weaker signal than a strong side project. Better to reframe than over-claim.
**Cited evidence:** See [../data/elyt-audit.md](../data/elyt-audit.md).

### T-02 — Elyt traction exceeds 500 users OR 1K GitHub stars within 6 months
**Action:** Reframe CV as "Founder & Principal Engineer, Elyt" (not "side project"). Shift M → D weighting; pursue remote-EU AI-engineer roles at €110K+ aggressively. Consider founding-engineer roles (Option K) at YC-backed AI startups with the traction data as diligence material.
**Reasoning:** Real traction = real product. Changes negotiation leverage substantially.

## Primary search triggers

### T-03 — Month 5 (October 2026): no onsite invites yet
**Action:** Profile is wrong. Iterate CV with explicit feedback from network contacts (from Month 3 coffee chats). Reassess: is it messaging (CV doesn't communicate the stacked M profile), market (roles don't exist this quarter), or candidate signal (actually underqualified for M)?
**Reasoning:** 5 months of structured search should produce at least 2 onsites if the profile fits the market. Zero onsites = broken somewhere in the funnel.

### T-04 — Month 6: offer at Browserbase / Parloa / Skyvern / Dynatrace Davis AI ≥ €100K
**Action:** Accept immediately. This is the M win state.
**Reasoning:** Target employer + target comp + target niche. Don't over-optimize.

### T-05 — Month 6: offer from AT-local platform-eng employer ≥ €80K
**Action:** Accept if no warm leads from network progressing. Negotiate aiming €85K. Bitpanda P50 €79K is the anchor.
**Reasoning:** AT platform floor secured; M-trajectory continues in-role.

### T-06 — Month 9: no offer ≥ €85K (primary stalls)
**Action:** Trigger F hedge. Start OSCP prep (~€1549). Pivot remaining 6 months' career-development time primarily to security track. Target NIS2 compliance-engineering roles at Erste / Raiffeisen / OMV / ÖBB for Spring 2027.
**Reasoning:** Primary has had full cycle to produce result. Opportunity cost of continuing to fish in M waters exceeds the ramp cost of switching to F.

## Macro triggers

### T-07 — AI-winter-lite materializes Q4 2026 or Q1 2027
**Indicator:** ≥ 3 major AI companies layoffs (Anthropic, OpenAI, DeepMind, Mistral) in one quarter, OR 30% drop in AI startup funding QoQ.
**Action:** Shift weighting: M remains primary but emphasize the Platform (E) leg over the Agent/AI (D) leg in CV framing. Increase F hedge effort from 15% to 30%. De-prioritize option K (founding-engineer).
**Reasoning:** Durability-heavy niches (platform, security) gain share; AI-native premium compresses.

### T-08 — EU economy enters recession (2 consecutive quarters negative GDP)
**Action:** Accept 10% lower comp targets. Prioritize stability over ceiling. Consider taking A (.NET) at a publicly-funded employer (Post AG, A1, Dynatrace) as risk hedge.
**Reasoning:** Enterprise .NET is counter-cyclical; AI startups are pro-cyclical.

## Personal triggers

### T-09 — Current employer (Axess) offers promotion to ≥ €78K with interesting work
**Action:** Evaluate: internal promotion vs external M offer. If the internal role includes platform / agent work → consider staying (KV-IT tenure compounds; low disruption). If it's more of the same .NET work → decline or use as leverage for M search.
**Reasoning:** Internal moves in DACH typically yield 5–10% lift; external moves 15–30%. But continuity has option value.

### T-10 — Candidate receives founding-engineer offer at YC-backed / Series-A-funded AI startup
**Action:** Evaluate carefully — equity value × mortality rate × learning value. If cash floor is acceptable (≥ €75K base) and company has ≥ 18 months runway and lead investor is top-tier (Sequoia, a16z, Benchmark, Lightspeed) → take it.
**Reasoning:** Compounding at the right AI-native startup in 2026 is a career-compressing bet.
