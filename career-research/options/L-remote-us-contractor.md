## Description
Register as Austrian self-employed (Gewerbe / Neue Selbständige). Contract with US companies.

## One-line verdict
Use as the *how* for paths D / E / K / M when the right US role appears — not as a separate plan.

## Tax/net reality
- Headline gross: $150–200K USD for senior contractor.
- AT SVS (~27% social contributions)
- AT income tax (up to 50%)
- VAT reverse-charge admin
- US W-8BEN filings
- **Net take-home: ~€75–100K from a $150K gross.** Not the Reddit-headline $150K.
- No Abfertigung Neu, 13./14. Monatsgehalt, Krankengeld, employer pension.

## Access-friendly US companies (remote-EU)
- Agent/AI: Browserbase, Skyvern, Stagehand, Factory, Lindy
- Platform: Vercel, Replicate, PlanetScale, Turso, Fly.io
- Observability: Grafana, Datadog (EU offices)

## Setup admin (approximate)
- Neue Selbständige registration: ~1 month
- Accountant: ~€2–3K/year
- First contract: 4–8 months of outbound

## AI-replacement risk
**Depends on the work.** Contractor is employment structure; inherits the underlying role's AI-resistance. Agent/platform contracting → 5/5. Generic CRUD contracting → 2/5.

## When to use
- When a remote-US offer appears for a role that matches M / D / K
- Not as a first-pass plan
