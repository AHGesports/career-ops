## What's deliberately NOT on the list (and why)

- **"Data Scientist"** — PhD-adjacent, wrong experience shape, saturated entry.
- **"ML Research / Scientist"** — Same; requires publications.
- **"Android / iOS mobile"** — no existing mobile experience; smaller pay ceiling in DACH than backend; heavily commoditized.
- **"Product Manager / TPM pivot"** — non-IC pivot, not what the candidate's CV supports, lower AI-resistance than assumed.
- **"Start your own company full-time"** — Elyt has unverified traction; no runway disclosed; out of scope of a career-path question.
- **"Management track"** — 22 is too early; IC premium in DACH is highest at staff IC, not at line-manager.

## Grouping by underlying bet

- **"Lang is fungible; pick any lane"** (A, B, C) — control cases that test whether prior-round "stay in lane" advice holds up.
- **"Niche moat beats language"** (E, F, G, H, I) — the real theses the fungibility premise enables.
- **"Elyt is the moat"** (D, K, M) — bets that monetize the solo-agentic-platform story.
- **"Geography arbitrage"** (L) — bets on leaving DACH comp entirely.
- **"Domain moat beats everything"** (J) — the fintech/regulated-industry thesis.
- **"Stacked scarcity"** (M) — the only hybrid; combines two theses to make a rarer profile.

Each is evaluated on the same criteria in [final.md](final.md).
