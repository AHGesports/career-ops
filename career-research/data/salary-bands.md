## Remote-US contractor — NET take-home for Austrian resident

**This is the most-asked-about and most-misquoted band.** Headline USD gross ≠ take-home EUR. Math below.

### Tax / social mechanics (Austria side, Neue Selbständige, 2026)

- Health (KV): **6.80%** of assessed income
- Pension (PV): **18.50%** — largest bite
- Self-Employed Provision (SV fund): **1.53%**
- Accident insurance: fixed €11.35/mo (~€136/yr)
- **Total SVS social (% of income):** ~**26.83%** above threshold
- Income tax (progressive 2026, after cold-progression relief): marginal rates 0% / 20% / 30% / 40% / 48% / 50% / 55% — high earners land effective ~35–45%
- VAT reverse-charge applies on B2B US invoices (admin burden, not additional tax at candidate's volume)
- Accountant: ~€2–3K/yr mandatory at this scale

**Simplified effective rate at $150K-€135K gross:** ~**27% SVS + ~32% effective income tax on taxable basis** = **~50–55% total take** → take-home ~€60–68K net.

**At $180K-€162K gross:** ~**28% SVS + ~36% effective income tax** = **~55–58% total take** → take-home ~€68–73K net.

**Source confidence:** HIGH on the SVS rates ([USP.gv.at](https://www.usp.gv.at/en/themen/mitarbeiter-und-gesundheit/einstellung-mitarbeiter-und-arten-der-beschaeftigung/weitere-informationen-einstellen-von-personal/neue-selbststaendige.html), [oida-austria.com](https://www.oida-austria.com/blog/income-taxes-in-austria-in-2025-what-you-should-know-as-a-self-employed-expat), [NEXORA tax guide 2026](https://nexora-consulting.at/en/taxes-payroll-costs-in-austria-2026-founders-guide-for-the-first-90-days/)); MEDIUM on the simplified effective-rate math (real number depends on deductions, GWG, 15% Gewinnfreibetrag).

**Critical finding:** A $150K US-contractor role for candidate yields ~**€60–68K NET take-home** — which is **equivalent to or below** a €78K AT salaried Platform Engineer when comparing net-of-tax. The "remote-US contractor arbitrage" that looks attractive on gross comp **only pays off at $180K+ USD, and primarily for AI/Agent-tier roles**.

**Additional losses not shown in net column:** no 13th/14th month (Urlaubsgeld + Weihnachtsgeld — that's ~14% of gross at a salaried AT role), no Abfertigung Neu, no Krankengeld from employer, no employer pension contributions, no paid sick leave, no Urlaub guarantee. These are real ~€5–10K/yr of implicit compensation that disappear under contractor structure.

**Practical conclusion:** Remote-US contractor pays off clearly at AI/Agent tier (Option D/M, $180K+ USD → €75K+ net). At other tiers the math is break-even or negative vs AT-salaried.

## Sample-size / reliability caveats

- **Levels.fyi AT submissions are thin** — Bitpanda, RBI, Dynatrace each have 20–80 submissions, not thousands. The bands are directionally right but individual percentile precision is ±10%.
- **karriere.at Gehalt pages** report mandatory KV floors plus employer self-reports; they systematically under-report actual comp by 10–20%.
- **Glassdoor** relies on anonymous user submissions with unverified seniority — dispersion tends to be wider than real market.
- **WeAreDevelopers Salary Report 2025** is the most reliable DACH-region primary source but lacks Austria-specific platform-engineer breakdown.
- **SalaryExpert** uses regression modeling — good for directional numbers, less reliable at the tails.

**When three or more sources agree within 10%, treat as HIGH confidence. When they disperse more, treat as MEDIUM.** Most tables above are HIGH/MEDIUM; none rely on single-source.
