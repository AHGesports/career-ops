## AT macro context

- **Unemployment (Aug 2025):** AT national 7.0%, Vienna 11.7%, Salzburg ~4–5%, Tyrol 3.4%
- **IT shortage:** ~20,000 unfilled IT positions in AT monthly. 78% of companies report skilled-worker shortages. "Softwareentwickler/in" on 2026 [Mangelberufe](https://www.wifi.at/blog/detail/1080-mangelberufe-oesterreich) list.
- **Germany:** 137,000 unfilled IT roles. 200 tech layoffs early 2026 (modest). 39% of DE software openings target mid-senior.
- **Implication:** mid-level devs in DACH are NOT in layoff crisis. They're in hiring-freeze-and-quiet-raise-freeze regime with real shortages for right profiles.
- **Risk:** stagnation, not unemployment.
